## 1. Executive Summary

Local governments in Nepal face significant challenges in managing citizen data, processing service requests, and maintaining transparent tax records. Manual, paper-based systems lead to delays, data loss, and citizen frustration.

**PalikaOS** is a next-generation, cloud-based Municipal Management System designed specifically to digitize and automate the core operations of Local Governments in Nepal. By adopting PalikaOS, [Municipality Name] will transition to a fully digital, paperless, and citizen-centric governance model.

This proposal outlines the deployment of PalikaOS as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) subscription, ensuring zero upfront infrastructure costs, continuous updates, and immediate deployment.

---

## 2. Key Objectives & Benefits

*   **100% Digitization:** Move away from paper registers (Darta Chalani) to a secure, searchable digital database.
*   **Citizen Convenience:** Citizens can track complaints, verify digital certificates, and receive SMS updates without visiting the ward office multiple times.
*   **Revenue Growth:** A dynamic, automated Tax Engine ensures accurate tax calculations, reducing human error and increasing municipal revenue collection.
*   **Zero IT Overhead:** Delivered as a fully managed cloud service. We handle the servers, backups, and security.

---

## 3. Core Modules Included

Your subscription to PalikaOS includes full access to the following integrated modules:

1.  **Ghatana Darta (Vital Events):** Digital registration and instant certificate generation for Births, Deaths, Marriages, and Migration. Includes verifiable QR codes.
2.  **Dynamic Tax & Revenue Engine:** Automated calculation of Property, Business, and Rent taxes based on rules set by your municipal assembly. Includes eSewa integration for digital payments.
3.  **Document Approvals (Sifaris):** Multi-stage digital approval workflows for official recommendations (Sifaris) with digital signatures.
4.  **Darta Chalani (Correspondence):** A complete digital registry for incoming and outgoing official letters with scanned PDF attachments.
5.  **Citizen Grievance & Service Requests:** A centralized dashboard to log, assign, and track citizen complaints ("Hello Mayor") and service requests.
6.  **Departmental Dashboards:** Specialized data tracking for Health, Education, Infrastructure, Agriculture, and Disaster Management departments.
7.  **Public Citizen Portal:** A white-labeled public website where citizens can track their application statuses using Tracking IDs.

## 5. Security & Data Sovereignty

*   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Strict permissions ensure that Ward Officers only see data relevant to their ward, while the Chief Administrative Officer (CAO) has a complete overview.
*   **Immutable Audit Logs:** Every action taken in the system is recorded (Who, What, When) to ensure total accountability and prevent fraud.
*   **Daily Backups & Encryption:** All citizen data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with automated daily backups to prevent data loss.
